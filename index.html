To Do Support

About

To Do is a mindful task manager that brings calm to your daily activities.

Features

  • Add tasks with the "+" button
  • Mark tasks complete by tapping the circle
  • Reorder tasks: long press and drag to rearrange
  • Delete tasks: swipe left on any task
  • All tasks automatically save to your device

Common Questions

  • Are my tasks backed up? Yes, all tasks are automatically saved to your device.
  • Can I sync across devices? Currently, tasks are stored locally on your device.
  • How do I delete completed tasks? Completed tasks can be removed by swiping left.
